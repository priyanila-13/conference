How to Reach the Venue?

Venue: Saranathan College of Engineering, Trichy, India
By Flight
  • The nearest airport to Saranathan College of Engineering is Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ), commonly known as Trichy Airport. You can book a flight to this airport from major cities in India.
  • Once you land at Trichy Airport, you'll be approximately 12-15 kilometers away from Saranathan College of Engineering.
  • After collecting your luggage, you can hire a taxi from the airport. Ride-sharing services like Ola and Uber are also available in Trichy. Tell the driver to take you to Saranathan College of Engineering, Panjappur.
  • The fastest route from the airport to the college is via National Highway 83 (NH83). The journey usually takes about 25-30 minutes, depending on traffic.
  • The taxi or ride-sharing vehicle will drop you at the entrance of Saranathan College of Engineering in Panjappur. The college is well-known locally, so it should be easy to locate.

Trichy International Airport

By Train
  • Board a train to Tiruchirappalli Junction (TPJ), the nearest major railway station.
  • Upon arrival at Tiruchirappalli Junction, exit the station and head towards the auto-rickshaw or taxi stand.
  • Hire an auto-rickshaw or taxi to Panjappur, the locality where the college is situated.
  • Alternatively, take a local bus from Tiruchirappalli Junction to Panjappur (ask for buses going towards Dindigul or Madurai route).
  • Get off at the Panjappur stop, which is the closest point to the college.
  • From Panjappur, hire a local auto-rickshaw for the short ride to Saranathan College of Engineering.
  • Follow the road signs or ask locals for directions to the college from Panjappur.
  • Arrive at Saranathan College of Engineering, which is located about 10 km from Tiruchirappalli Junction.

Trichy Railway Station

By Bus
  • Reach Trichy Central Bus Stand by any intercity or intracity bus.
  • Take a bus heading towards Dindigul or Madurai from the Central Bus Stand.
  • Get off at the Panjappur bus stop, which is located on the Trichy-Madurai National Highway (NH45).
  • Cross the highway to reach the road leading towards the college.
  • Walk or take an auto-rickshaw from the Panjappur bus stop to Saranathan College of Engineering, approximately 1.5 km away

Central Bus Stand - Trichy

By Car
  • Start on NH83 (Chennai-Trichy Highway) heading towards Trichy.
  • Take the exit onto NH81 towards Panjappur.
  • Continue straight and take a right onto the service road near Panjappur Junction.
  • Follow the road signs to Saranathan College of Engineering, located on the right.
